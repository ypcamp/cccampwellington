What do I need to know

Camp details

The camp will begin on the Friday 26th of August at 6pm. Please note that there will be no dinner provided on Friday night so be sure to have dinner before arriving. The first meeting will start from between 7 - 7:30pm. The camp will conclude at 1:30pm on the Monday 29th of August.

How much will it cost?

Camp fees will be dependent on your choice of accommodation. There will be two options available. The cheaper option, for $135, includes basic bunkroom accommodation. The second option, for $160, is a more comfortable option in pleasant villa and lodge accommodation.

What to bring

Here are some suggestions on what to bring for the weekend:

  • Blankets or sleeping bag, bed-sheet and pillow
  • Personal towel and toiletries
  • Sufficient warm clothing
  • Shoes for recreational activities
  • Bible and hymnals
  • Pen and notebook
  • Transport details

If you are planning to drive, aim to arrive at the camp no later than 6pm on Friday to register and receive room allocations. We encourage everyone to arrive early so that we can have a good beginning to the camp.

The Victoria University Christian Club will also provide a free transport from the airport and the meeting hall, 258 The Terrace, to and from the camp.

For those that need transport to the campsite, there will be two departure times. Transport from the airport to the meeting hall will be provided for flights before 2pm and transport from the meeting hall will leave promptly at 3pm on Friday. We advise that flights into Wellington airport should arrive no later than 2pm on Friday if you want transport to the campsite.

On the way back, we will provide transport to Wellington airport for those whose flights are later than 5pm.

Hospitality details

There is a limited amount of hospitality available if you wish to stay with some families located in the Wellington and Hutt City area before and/or after the camp. Please let us know if you require hospitality at least 3 weeks before the camp begins.

Who to contact

For any other general enquiries and payment details about the camp, contact the local coordinator in your area. Alternatively, you can also email us.
